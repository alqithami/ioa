Internet of Agents

The First International Workshop on the Internet of Agents (IoA)

WI Conference Paper Submission

Omaha, Nebraska United States

October 13, 2016

Workshop Program:


Program Overview:
  • The workshop will feature invited talks by leading researchers and an exciting collection of oral presentations as well as an interactive poster session.
  • Oral presentations will be followed by talks from our sponsors about current and future applications of the IoA in industry and more in-person discussions at the poster session.
  • The panel discussion will take place after paper presentations and will have a rather impromptu nature. It will discuss important issues related to workshop aim and to answer some important questions, such as:
      — What is expected from IoA?
      — What are some of the general challenges facing IoA and potential solutions?
      — Are there available applications for reserchers persuing IoA field?
      - If you would like to contribute to this panel and may have more questions/ideas to discuss, please let us hear from you.
  • At the closing, we will announce the winner (if any is nominated!) for the two awards: an outstanding paper award and the best poster award.
